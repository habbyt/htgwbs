Biography

I am a GIS Specialist in Helsinki, Finland with with extensive experience working in governmental organizations, private companies and educational institutions. I have a Masters degree in GeoInformatics from Aalto university and a professional Masters degree in Geomatics and Natural Resource Evaluation from Istituto agronomico per l'oltremare (Florence University).

I am skilled in analysis, modeling and visualization of Geographic data including aerial and satellite imageries. I am experienced in most commercial and open source GIS softwares such as ArcGIS and QGIS.

I enjoy teamwork and close collaborations as much as independent work. I am keen to learn and excel more. I have an agreeable personality with good verbal and written communication skills.

I am interested in providing GIS solutions to environmental, financial, developmental and emerging sectors with geospatial needs.
